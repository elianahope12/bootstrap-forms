Selena Gomez Quiz

Selena Gomez is a young actress and pop singer. She started her career on Barney and Friends as a child actress and then eventually starred in Wizards of Waverly Place on Disney Channel. She is now a very successful singer, song writer and became a UNICEF ambassador at the age of 17. Her relationship with Justin Bieber received a lot of media attention in the mid 2000s but today it is her friendship with powerful women like Demi Lovato and Taylor Swift that get most of her social headlines.
